Data Scientist

A Data Scientist is a professional who utilizes a combination of statistical, mathematical, and programming skills to extract valuable insights and knowledge from large sets of data. These insights are crucial for informing business decisions, solving complex problems, and discovering trends that may not be immediately apparent. The role of a data scientist involves various tasks, including data cleaning and preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, and the development of machine learning models.
